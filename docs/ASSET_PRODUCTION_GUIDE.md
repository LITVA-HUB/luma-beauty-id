# Asset Production Guide

Current bundled product-card visuals are generated packshot cards for the synthetic development/staging catalog. They are legal-safe but not final retail photography.

## Before release

- Replace editorial hero/onboarding visuals with licensed final assets.
- Replace synthetic `/assets/cards/` product cards with licensed product images from the production catalog/CDN.
- Keep fallback vector/product-shape visuals for network failures, but label seed/local data in non-production.

## Requirements

- No faces or hands unless releases and consent are secured.
- No readable fake packaging text.
- No fake logos or brand marks.
- No medical imagery.
- No neon/cyber AI glow.
- No scraped product photography.
- Use warm ivory/champagne/blush/lime palette and premium daylight styling.

## QA

Check each asset at 1x/2x/3x, light/dark decisions, small iPhone sizes and VoiceOver-hidden decorative usage.
