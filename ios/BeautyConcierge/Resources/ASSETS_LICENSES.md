# Asset Licenses

Current bundled visuals were generated programmatically as generic vector/raster placeholders inside this repository.

Allowed current use:

- development;
- staging;
- fallback/error/empty states;
- internal QA.

Not final without review:

- App Store marketing screenshots;
- real product photography;
- brand/catalog imagery.

Production requirements:

- licensed product images from catalog/CDN;
- licensed editorial imagery or internally produced photography;
- no copied third-party logos/assets;
- no readable fake package text;
- no medical imagery.
